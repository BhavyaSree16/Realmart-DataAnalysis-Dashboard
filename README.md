# Realmart-DataAnalysis-Dashboard
This project presents an interactive Super Market Sales Dashboard created using Power BI to analyze sales performance across different cities, product lines, and payment methods. The dashboard provides valuable insights into revenue, customer ratings, and product performance to support data-driven decision making.
## Key Questions(KPI's) 
The dashboard answers the following business questions:
1. What is the total income generated by the supermarket?
2. What is the total quantity of products sold?
3. What is the average customer rating?
4. Which city generates the highest revenue?
5. What is the gross income by payment method and city?
6. Which payment method contributes the most to sales?
7. Which product line generates the highest income?
8. What is the average customer rating by product line?
9. How does gross margin percentage vary across payment methods?
## Process
The following steps were followed to build the dashboard:
Data Collection : Imported the supermarket sales dataset into Power BI.
Data Cleaning : Checked for missing values and ensured correct data types and Organized columns for better analysis.
Data Modeling : Structured the dataset to enable effective filtering and aggregation.
Dashboard Development : Created KPI cards for Total Income, Total Quantity, and Average Rating.
Built multiple visualizations including:
1. Bar charts
2. Line charts
3. Pie charts
4. Tables
5. Slicers for filtering by city and product line.
## Project Insights
Key insights derived from the dashboard:
1. Mumbai generates the highest sales income compared to other cities.
2. Food and Beverages contribute the highest gross income among product categories.
3. Cash payment method dominates transactions, followed by credit card and e-wallet.
4. Fashion accessories receive the highest average rating from customers.
5. Some product categories show lower customer satisfaction, indicating areas for improvement.
## Final Conclusion
The Super Market Sales Dashboard provides a clear overview of sales performance and customer behavior.By analyzing revenue, product demand, payment preferences, and customer ratings, businesses this can make better strategic decisions to improve sales, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency.
